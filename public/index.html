Welcome to Charity Box!

A Charity Box consists of basic things that every child needs: such as food, school supplies and toys.
For us, these things aren’t any special but sadly this isn’t the case for everybody in this world.

We work together with a lot of other organizations that help us to get the Charity Box to the right place.

After making a box, we keep stay in contact with you! We really want to make a bond and keep you up to date with everything we do!
If the people who receive the box are willing to let us take a picture of them together with their new goodies, you will receive that picture on your profile!
